Restaurant History

Real Special Taste, Rooted in Golden Tradition

Stackly Restaurant was born from a passion for slow-cooked flavors and handcrafted recipes. From our first brick oven to our bustling open kitchen today, we’ve always believed in doing things the right way—fresh ingredients, bold spices, and attention to every single plate.

Our black-and-gold interior tells the story of classic elegance meeting modern energy, making Stackly the perfect place for family dinners, business lunches, and late-night celebrations.

  • Artisan-crafted menu inspired by global street food and fine dining.
  • Locally sourced ingredients with seasonal specialties every month.
  • Dedicated chefs and mixologists curating unforgettable experiences.
