Our Story

About Stackly Restaurant

Stackly started as a small late‑night pizza joint and grew into a full‑service black‑and‑gold dining destination loved by families, foodies, and travelers.

We mix comfort food nostalgia with fine‑dining techniques to create a menu that feels both familiar and exciting.

Values

What Matters Most to Us

Honest Ingredients

We build relationships with local farmers, butchers, and bakers so we always know where our ingredients come from.

Warm Hospitality

Every guest is treated like a regular. We remember your favorites and celebrate your moments with you.

Creative Craft

Our chefs constantly experiment with flavors, plating, and textures to keep the menu evolving.

More Than a Restaurant

Stackly is a stage for unforgettable evenings, where fire, flavor, and fine hospitality meet.

Timeline

From One Oven to a Full Kitchen

2011

Stackly opens as a small pizza spot with just 20 seats and a single wood‑fired oven.

2015

We expand the menu to include burgers, salads, and desserts, plus a new open kitchen layout.

2019

The black‑and‑gold concept is introduced, with a complete interior redesign and new chef’s tasting menus.

2024

Stackly becomes a go‑to venue for events, hosting weddings, corporate dinners, and food festivals.
